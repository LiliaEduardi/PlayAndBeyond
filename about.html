About Play & Beyond Fun Zone

Our Belief at Play & Beyond Fun Zone is that children and adults should be surrounded by Love and Laughter. We created a space where little ones with their family and friends can explore, relax, and enjoy their time together. Our Indoor Playground features two play structures for gross motor skill development and a toddler area that allows younger children to play safely.

At Play and Beyond, parents can relax, have a coffee and enjoy free Wi-Fi access while their little ones are playing. The surrounding areas support creativity, imaginative play, and learning.

Play & Beyond Fun Zone Vision

Play & Beyond Fun Zone not only wants to be a part of little, day-to-day moments but also big special celebrations (Birthday Parties, Baby Showers, Family Gatherings, etc). We look forward to transforming our playground for scheduled events customized to your taste. We hope Play & Beyond Fun Zone will provide an environment where all will feel comfortable and cared for.

And Remember - Your Happiness Is Our Success!
